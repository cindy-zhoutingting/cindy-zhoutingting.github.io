About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at [Data Science and Engineering](https://dase.ecnu.edu.cn/main.htm), [East China Normal University](http://www.ecnu.edu.cn/en/), advised by [Peng Cai](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=NEwtleUAAAAJ). I am also a research intern at TiDB cloud platform team, [PingCAP](https://www.pingcap.com/). I was fortunate to work closely with Prof. [Xuan Zhou](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=YcJn26QAAAAJ) and Prof. [Aoying Zhou](https://dblp.org/pid/z/AoyingZhou.html).
